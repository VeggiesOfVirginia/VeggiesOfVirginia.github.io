2nd Annual Vegan Mac n' Cheese Cook-off

Veggies of Virginia's annual Vegan Mac n' Cheese Cook-off brings together the UVa and Charlottesville community for an evening celebrating the delicious power of plant based food. Students and community members compete against each other to see who has the best vegan mac n' cheese recipe. Attendees and a panel of judges will get to taste all of the dishes and vote on their favorite mac n' cheese. There will be prize baskets awarded to the winners of judges choice and peoples choice, plus there will be a $20 Roots gift card raffle for the attendees. Register to compete with your own vegan mac n' cheese or buy a ticket ($5 in advance or $7 at the door) to taste. Check out the Facebook event here.
